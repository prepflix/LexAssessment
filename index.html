About Infosys

Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational corporation that provides business consulting, information technology, and outsourcing services. It was founded in 1981 by seven engineers in Pune, India, and is headquartered in Bangalore, India.

Services

Infosys offers a wide range of services, including:

  • Application Development and Maintenance
  • Business Process Outsourcing
  • Consulting Services
  • Digital Transformation Services
  • Engineering Services
  • Enterprise Mobility
  • Infrastructure Management Services
  • Software Development

Global Presence

Infosys operates globally, with offices and delivery centers in multiple countries including the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, and several other countries across Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Infosys is committed to sustainability and corporate social responsibility. The company actively engages in initiatives that focus on education, health, and environmental sustainability. Infosys Foundation, the philanthropic arm of Infosys, supports various programs aimed at improving the lives of underprivileged communities.

Achievements

Infosys has received numerous awards and accolades for its innovative solutions and business practices. It is recognized as a leader in IT services by various industry analysts and has been included in prestigious lists such as Forbes Global 2000 and Fortune India 500.
